墨竹

2024.03-2024.07

AI生成设计、数字艺术

墨竹作品融合了传统水墨画技法与AI生成艺术，通过对竹字草书进行转译，生成了一把草书椅。这一系列作品探索了传统书法与现代人工智能的结合，展现了东方美学在当代科技语境下的新可能性。

The Ink Bamboo project combines traditional ink painting techniques with AI-generated art, translating the cursive script of the Chinese character for "bamboo" into a chair design. This series explores the intersection of traditional calligraphy and modern artificial intelligence, revealing new possibilities for Eastern aesthetics in contemporary technological contexts.

竹编亭

2024.03-2024.07

竹编空间装置、AIGC设计、机械臂建造

竹编亭项目通过对传统竹编纹样进行转译，用AI生图技术将二维的编织纹样变成三维的竹编亭，最后用机械臂实际建造出来。这一系列作品探索了传统工艺、人工智能与数字制造的结合，创造出既具实用功能又富有艺术美感的空间结构，展现了竹子作为建筑材料的无限可能性。

The Bamboo Pavilion project translates traditional bamboo weaving patterns, using AI image generation technology to transform 2D weaving patterns into 3D pavilion structures, which are then physically constructed using robotic arms. This series explores the combination of traditional craftsmanship, artificial intelligence, and digital manufacturing, creating spatial structures with both practical functionality and artistic aesthetics, showcasing the unlimited possibilities of bamboo as a building material.

吃定你了

2025.07

RAG、智能推荐系统

“吃定你了”是一个利用AI技术解决“吃什么”难题的餐厅推荐网站。它提供四大核心功能：用随机转盘终结选择困难；通过智能推荐标签筛选高分档口；查阅客观的排行榜；以及与星星助手对话，直接询问档口评分和美食推荐。我们通过前期收集的用户真实评价构建专属知识库，当用户提问或筛选时，系统会先从中精准检索相关信息，再由AI模型生成回答。确保所有推荐都基于真实用户反馈，提供了高度个性化和可信的决策参考。

"Eatimate" is an AI-powered restaurant recommendation website designed to solve the dilemma of "where to eat." It offers four core features: a random wheel to end indecisiveness, smart recommendation tags for filtering highly-rated stalls, an objective ranking list, and a conversation with Star Assistant to directly inquire about stall ratings and food recommendations.By building a dedicated knowledge base from collected authentic user reviews, the system first retrieves relevant information accurately when users ask questions or apply filters. Then, the AI model generates responses based on this data, ensuring that all recommendations are grounded in real user feedback, providing highly personalized and trustworthy decision-making support.

Leveraging AIGC capabilities, the platform automates the entire process from global trend-based product selection and marketing script generation to promotional material optimization. With the support of multilingual digital human anchors, it provides 24/7 unmanned livestreaming services across time zones and in multiple languages. This solution effectively addresses the challenges of high costs, low efficiency, and cross-border operations in traditional livestreaming, helping brands efficiently reach the global market and driving the intelligent and global evolution of e-commerce livestreaming.
